about mewt.

mewt was founded by two BITS Pilani alumni partners: Rishabh Jain and Kushal Prakash,

mewt - a fin-tech Indian startup was founded on July 2021 in Bangalore, India. mewt is focused to make SME's self sufficient & have all their banking needs in one single app. mewt also rewards its user & provides range of benefits, including discounts on financial services, access to exclusive events, and more.

We're on a mission to empower SMEs to grow their businesses and increase revenue through seamless and effortless transfers, changing their relationship with how they handle money.

mewt aims to provide small businesses with the tools they need to take control of their finances, manage their accounts in one place, and make informed decisions about their financial futures.
